Speeds up Supply Chain

We absorb the extra food from the kitchens, hotels and canteens at very less time, with all care taken. We bring them for quality testing and within minutes supply them to the needy in the close vicinity. We have also optimised NGOs by supplying them food at blazing fast rate while maintaining the food's hygiene.

We value our volunteers and end users who form a crucial part of this mission. We understand what hunger means. Thus, we make every attempt to reach your location within 25 minutes of order. We do have some emotional surprises for our donors.
